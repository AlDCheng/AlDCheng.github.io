Featured project

Low power time-of-flight imaging for augmented reality

Depth sensors are useful for many mobile augumented reality (AR) applications, but are very power hungry, and can limit the battery-life of the underlying device. We experiment with using depth estimation, where subsequent color images are used to determine camera motion in order to estimate new depth maps. Furthermore, we construct an AR pipeline showcasing the use of depth estimation.
